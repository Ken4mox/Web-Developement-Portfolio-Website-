About

Blending partnerships, delivery, and tech curiosity.

I’m Kennedy Mokaya Asiago, a Strategic Project and Partnerships Manager based in Nairobi. For over eight years I’ve worked with governments, NGOs, and private sector teams to design and deliver programs across East Africa.

My work sits where strategy meets execution. I help teams structure programs, align stakeholders, and build the routines needed to move from concept notes to working digital tools.

Alongside this, I’m growing my technical skills in web development and digital tools – so I can better understand what it takes to ship products and support the teams that build them.
