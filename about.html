About

Digital health, delivery, and systems improvement.

I’m Kennedy Mokaya Asiago, a Digital Health & Software Project Manager based in Nairobi. I work with counties, government teams, and partners to deploy and sustain digital systems used by community health workers and supervisors across Kenya.

My focus is helping teams move from strategy to working systems - translating policies, workflows, and frontline needs into practical digital tools that improve service delivery, data quality, and performance across regions.

I’m also building deeper technical skills in software development and data so I can better support engineering teams, lead UAT processes, and guide system improvements grounded in both technical and user realities.

I’m especially interested in roles that combine leadership, digital transformation, and government engagement - including Digital Health Manager roles.
